>>> Lori's Secret Cow Level! <<<

>>> Features:
> Endless horde of 2000 cows!
> Eat fruits to survive longer!

>>> Controls:
> WASD - Move
> L/R Arrows - Rotate the firing direction
> Mouse - Adjust firing direction (overrides L/R arrows)

>>> Pickups
> apple: + HP
> banana: + Firing spread
> eggplant: + Firing speed
> pineapple: SuperMode!!

>>> Enemies
> B&W: Weakest
> Brown: Healthiest
> Blue: Most score

>>> Notes
> No screen transitions yet.. Game just ends when you run out of HP!
(HP display won't even be updated to 0 >_<)
> No sounds yet..
> Horribly slow on Debug mode >_<
